# EpistemicLab RA Coverage Matrix

**Baseline:** 578 SBA, 360 enriched SBA, 276 micro-drills, 106 OR, 6 SAT,
20 operational misconception nodes, and 199 causal chains.

## Method

Counts are deterministic, multi-label evidence matches. One object may support
multiple subtopics, so column totals must not be summed. `Coach` counts explicit
matched coaching assets or misconception coaching paths. `Ready` counts
explicit readiness-oriented support; zero does not mean no general feedback.

Classification:

- **EXCELLENT:** broad, repeated, multimodal evidence with written and causal support.
- **STRONG:** reliable depth with only a secondary modality gap.
- **ADEQUATE:** usable coverage, but limited rotation or one important modality absent.
- **WEAK:** sparse, recognition-heavy, or metadata-uncertain coverage.
- **MISSING:** no defensible mapped evidence.
- **UNKNOWN:** the source cannot be mapped confidently without human review.

OR counts use content evidence, not only `ra_id`. Recorded RA3-RA5 labels are
frequently inconsistent with official RA definitions; questionable items are
not credited merely because of their label.

## Mapping limitations

- Two SBA records have an unmapped RA value and are excluded from RA quotas.
- OR topic labels after `OR_037` do not consistently represent their recorded
  RA. Content-based matches are credited only where defensible.
- Generic causal-chain text makes an exact palate-structure chain count
  over-inclusive; it remains `UNKNOWN`.
- Coaching counts measure explicit support, not the quality of the rendered
  learner experience.
