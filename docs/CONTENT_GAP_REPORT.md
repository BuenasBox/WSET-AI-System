# EpistemicLab Content Gap Report

**Audit date:** 2026-06-15  
**Constraint:** Quantification only. No content was created or modified.

## 1. How estimates were calculated

Two estimates are used:

- **Minimum coverage floor:** enough additions to remove hard-missing cells and give weak high-value cells at least one usable pathway.
- **Balanced operating target:** enough additions for repeated practice and format diversity, generally two examples per high-priority weak cell.

These are content-object estimates, not examiner marks. One well-designed object may cover more than one subtopic, so ranges are more honest than a single false-precision number.
