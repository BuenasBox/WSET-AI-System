WSET Level 3 Award in Wines mock tasting exam

Instructions (please read carefully)

•  This pack contains six wines divided into three mock exams
•  For each examination you must describe and assess two still

wines, one white and one red

•  You may assess the wines in any order
•  The three mock exams are divided as following:

o  Mock A (Wines 1 & 2)
o  Mock B (Wines 3 & 4)
o  Mock C (Wines 5 & 6)

•  Write your descriptions and conclusions in the spaces provided
•  You may NOT refer to notes or any other reference material

(including the SAT) during exam conditions

•  You have 30 minutes to complete each mock examination
•  The marking keys are available through the QR code or link:

bit.ly/3VAGbSg
