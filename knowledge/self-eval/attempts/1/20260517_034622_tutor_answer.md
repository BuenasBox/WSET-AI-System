## 3. Explicación causa → efecto
**CAUSA:** Flor yeast (a film of Saccharomyces cerevisiae strains) forms on the surface of the wine in partially filled barrels
**MECANISMO:** The flor layer provides oxygen protection by excluding oxygen from the wine surface; the yeast metabolises ethanol and glycerol, producing acetaldehyde, and undergoes autolysis adding amino acids and breadlike compounds
**EFECTO:** The wine develops biological ageing character: almond, bread dough, and yeasty notes, low colour, low tannin, protected from oxidation despite barrel ageing
**FORMULACIÓN DE EXAMEN:** Flor yeast provides oxygen protection, producing biological ageing character: almond and bread dough flavours develop while oxidation is prevented by the flor layer.

*Relevancia SAT: Use when tasting or discussing Fino or Manzanilla sherry style. Biological ageing character (almond, bread, low colour) is a product of flor protection, distinct from oxidative ageing aromas.*
